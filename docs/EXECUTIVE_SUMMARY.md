## 🏥 The Problem

Healthcare facilities across East Africa lose revenue and efficiency every day to preventable system failures:

| Challenge | Impact |
|:--|:--|
| **Revenue leakage** | Services rendered but never billed — labs, radiology, pharmacy dispensing — slip through the cracks, costing facilities 15–30% of potential revenue |
| **Paper-based chaos** | Patient files get lost, prescriptions are illegible, and clinical history is incomplete — risking patient safety and wasting hours of staff time |
| **Disconnected systems** | Billing doesn't talk to pharmacy. Lab doesn't talk to the ward. Insurance claims are processed manually. Every handoff is a point of failure |
| **Internet dependency** | Cloud-based systems go offline when the network drops — and in East Africa, it drops often. Patient care cannot wait for a stable connection |
