Frequently asked

How does the free trial work?

Professional includes a 14-day free trial; Enterprise includes a 30-day pilot. No credit card is required to start — you'll only be billed if you choose to keep the plan after the trial.

Can we host it ourselves?

Yes — Enterprise can be self-hosted on your own AWS / Azure / on-prem infrastructure. Air-gapped (USB-distributed updates) is available on request.

What about NHIF and other insurance schemes?

Professional and Enterprise include the insurance claims module with NHIF (Uganda/Kenya) and customizable schema for other payers.

Do you offer training?

Professional includes remote onboarding. Enterprise includes on-site training, data migration, and ongoing change-management support.

Is patient data secure?

All deployments use encrypted backups, JWT auth with short-lived tokens, role-based access, and full audit logging. See our architecture docs.
